Privacy

May we collect the following anonymous data about your use of Firefox Relay?

  • When the add-on pop-up is viewed
  • When a button or link in the add-on is clicked
  • When the Relay icon is injected into website forms
  • When a new alias is created
  • When the add-on modal is viewed or closed

We collect this data to help us determine what is working, and which pieces of functionality need improvement. There is no penalty for choosing not to allow us to collect anonymous data.

Full details about the anonymous data we collect and what we do with it are provided in our Privacy Policy.
